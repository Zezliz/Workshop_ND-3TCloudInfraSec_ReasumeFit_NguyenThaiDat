### Areas for Improvement

*   **Enhancing troubleshooting skills (Debugging):** When encountering complex authorization issues (IAM Policies) or connectivity failures between services, I sometimes feel overwhelmed and spend considerable time searching for answers. I need to learn how to analyze system logs (such as CloudWatch) in a more systematic and logical manner.
*   **Optimizing hands-on deployment:** Although I understand the theories and deployment steps, I still need to practice configuring labs multiple times to build speed and reduce minor configuration mistakes.
*   **Proactively networking and communicating:** Despite feeling more confident after attending FCAJ Community Day events, I still need to take more initiative in talking to and discussing deep technical topics with cloud experts and seniors to gain more real-world, hands-on experience.
